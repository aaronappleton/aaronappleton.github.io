ensigo · Kamuwe Ekirabo

Ensigo is an ongoing project that pairs musicians and visual artists from across East Africa and North America to produce works that expand the boundaries of artistic expression. The resulting music and accompanying artwork are then sold to fund arts-based grants as a way to support the growth of the creative economies in East Africa.

Audio-Based Games Platform

Imagine a first-person adventure game...but instead of needing to be glued to a screen to guide an avatar around a virtual space with your hand, YOU are the protagonist...in the real world. I developed a mobile game platform (for smartwatch or smartphone) where anyone can make or play audio-based games. Games only plays while the listener is moving. The app detects your location, tracks your speed, and simulates missions where you need to do physical movements or respond with verbal commands in order to progress in the game. This audio-first platform provides a counterpoint to the highly produced, one-way nature of audiobooks and podcasts, and the sedentary nature of screen-based games.

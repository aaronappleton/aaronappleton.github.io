SciX

During my masters program at Harvard I went through a human-centered design process to create this UI prototype for a platform that helps knowledge workers keep up to date with their research interests as they evolve in real time. The platform does this through a combination of artificial and human intelligence to analyze and aggregate millions of newly published research articles, and then create feeds tailored to our users unique interests.



Role: Design Director (UI/UX, User Research)

SMALLab Games

SMALLab (opens new window) is a room-sized embodied learning environment that integrates motion-capture technology with immersive floor projection. Our game-based experiences blend digital and physical worlds to invent new kinds of playful learning. Building off the software originally created by the Institute of Play, I redesigned three SMALLab games for science and math students attending the Manhattan school Quest to Learn.

Role: Lead creative technologist in SMALLab games redesign

In Collaboration With: Institute of Play

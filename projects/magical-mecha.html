Magical Gurl Mecha

A cooperative, anime-inspired space adventure

This project was an assignment for New Arcade (opens new window), a class focused on making unconventional controllers for video games. For this project, we tried to make a game centered around flying a magical robot with a friend through custom motion controls.

Role: OpenCV programming and OSC protocol

In collaboration with: Sydney Adams (opens new window) (Design Lead), Taeyeon Kim (opens new window) (Art Assets), and Seungho Jung (opens new window) (Unity Programming)

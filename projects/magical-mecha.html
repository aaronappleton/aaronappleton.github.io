Playful Learning Design

Playful Learning Design is a physical game for guiding cross-functional design teams through a journey of discovery, development, and delivery of educational products. Players venture through a number of individual and group-based creative challenges that must be completed to build out their teams map. All these mini-design challenges add up to a fully developed product that's ready to be launched by the end of the game.

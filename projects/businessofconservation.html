The Business of Conservation

The Business of Conservation is a global conference that brings together leaders and passionate individuals from the conservation sector, and beyond, to explore how wildlife conservation can serve as a key pillar of sustainable economic development and innovation. Our goal with this gathering is to create a coalition of leaders who understand that conservation is one of our global economy's greatest competitive advantages.



Role: Led the design of conference sessions and activities

Methods Used: Human-centered design, learning experience design, subject matter expert interviews, user journey mapping

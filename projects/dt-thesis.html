ensigo

ensigo · City of Peace Remember This City of War

Ensigo is an ongoing project that pairs musicians and visual artists from across East Africa and North America to produce works that expand the boundaries of artistic expression. The resulting music and accompanying artwork are then sold to fund arts-based grants as a way to stimulate the creative economies in East Africa.

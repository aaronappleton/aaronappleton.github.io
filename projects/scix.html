SciX

During graduate studies at Harvard I designed this prototype for a product that helps knowledge workers keep up to date with their research interests as they evolve in real time. The product does this through a combination of artificial and human intelligence to analyze and aggregate millions of newly published research articles, and then create feeds tailored to our users unique interests.



Role: Lead Designer

Tools Used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Figma

Methods Used: Learning experience design, UI/UX design, cognitive task analysis, jobs to be done interviews, subject matter expert interviews, rapid prototyping, usability testing, desirability testing, work domain analysis

Hello! I'm Aaron, an applied learning scientist working in R&D for learning.

I’m wild about leveraging breakthrough insights from the sciences of how people learn, and rigorously applying them to the design of first of a kind programs and products that generate the greatest impact on the development of adaptive expertise.

Whether designing at the scale of a tabletop game for scientist entrepreneurs, a room-sized narrative environment for medical students, or an immersive experience that takes over a city borough for intelligence officers—I aim to craft a playable future of learning that pushes the boundaries of human expertise.

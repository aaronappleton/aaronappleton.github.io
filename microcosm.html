Microcosm

What happens when we as human beings confront a world that is radically unhuman, impersonal, and even indifferent to the human?

This project was an entry in the Philly Game Forge's Tiny Jam in 2016. The primary constraint was that the project could only use 10KB of code.

Using similar Newtonian physics to my Space Is Flat project, i made an asteroids-like game where the player has to orbit a field of planetoids as long as possible without crashing.
